Privacy Policy for Minimal Mind

Last Updated: March 17, 2025

At 51Miles, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our "Minimal Mind" app, a simple meditation timer designed to support your mindfulness practice. In short: we don't collect or store any personal data from you.

Information We Collect

We designed Minimal Mind to be simple and distraction-free, and we don't collect or store any personal information about you. The app operates entirely on your device, and no data is sent to us or any third parties.

Features and Data Usage

Here's how Minimal Mind interacts with your device:

Meditation Timer and Settings: All features, such as session duration, interval bells, and ambient nature sounds (forest, rain, nature), are stored and processed locally on your device. Nothing is transmitted off your device.

Apple Health Integration: Minimal Mind can log your meditation sessions as "Mindful Minutes" or "Mind & Body" workouts in the Apple Health app. This is optional and controlled by you. We only write data (e.g., session duration) to Apple Health and do not read or access any data from it. This integration uses Apple's HealthKit framework, and all data remains on your device under your control.

Daily Reminders: Notifications and motivational messages are handled locally on your device. We don't use external servers or services to deliver them.

No Tracking or Analytics

Minimal Mind does not use any form of tracking, analytics, or crash reporting. We don't collect usage statistics, device information, or any other data about how you use the app.

Third Parties

We don't share your data with third parties because we don't collect any data to share. All app functionality is self-contained on your device.

Children's Privacy

Minimal Mind is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with information (though this is unlikely given our app's design), please contact us at support@51miles.nl.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. If we make changes, we'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
