Q: Why isn't the app syncing with Apple Health?
A: Please ensure you've granted minimal mind. permission to access Health data. Go to your device's Settings > Privacy > Health > minimal mind. and enable permissions.

Q: How do I set up daily reminders?

Technical Support

Installation Issues

If you're having trouble installing minimal mind., please try the following:

  • Check that your device is running iOS 14.0 or later
  • Ensure you have enough storage space on your device
  • Restart your device and try downloading again
  • Make sure your Apple ID is active and in good standing

Health Integration

To enable Apple Health integration:

  1. Open the Settings app on your device
  2. Navigate to Privacy > Health
  3. Find minimal mind. in the list of apps
  4. Ensure "Mindful Minutes" is toggled ON

Notification Setup

If you're not receiving reminders:

  1. Check that notifications are enabled in the app settings
  2. Go to your device's Settings > Notifications > minimal mind.
  3. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is toggled ON
  4. Make sure your device is not in Do Not Disturb mode during reminder times

Sound Problems

If you're experiencing issues with sounds in the app:

  • Ensure your device is not on silent mode
  • Check the volume level using the volume buttons
  • Try using headphones to see if the issue persists
  • Restart the app and your device

Contact Technical Support

If you're still experiencing issues, please contact our technical support team at techsupport@minimalmind.app with the following information:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • App version (found in Settings > About)
  • A detailed description of the issue
  • Any error messages you've received
  • Steps you've already taken to resolve the issue
